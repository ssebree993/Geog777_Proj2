# 2025 Mustang Island State Park (MSP) Guide

[Find Mustang Island Activities](https://github.com/ssebree993/Geog777_Proj2) 2025 Mustang Island State Park (MSP) Guide

## Introduction
People visting the Corpus Christi Texasmay be interested in visting a convenenient location for outdoor activity. The Mustang Island State Park (MSP) managed by the Texas State Parks and Wildlife Department (TPWD) offers a variety of activities for families with children and is dog friendly.  Kite flying, kayaking, walking on the beach, fishing, camping and swimming are a few of the opportunities available to soak up some sun. The MSP guide is an application for the general visitor to gain a better understanding of where park features located in relation to a stay in the Corpus Christi / Port Aransas area. People will be able to report points of interest, take photos of wildlife and view other visitor's picture and comments through this application and Survey123.

## Features
- Interactive webmap with a points of interest layer and popup information showing photos
- Kayak trails symbolized by the name of the trail and three launch locations
- Find the campground and beach tent sites
- Locate the beach, dunes and marine marsh areas on a base map layer
- Add your own sighting using Survey123, accessible through plus button


## Technology Stack

**Front-end:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap

**Mapping Libraries and APIs:** Esri.js, ArcGIS Maps SDK for JavaScript

**Back-end:** ArcGIS Online

**Geospatial Database:** Hosted Feature Layers on AGOL

**Data Collection:** Survey123
